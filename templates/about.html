When I first got my A Levels results 2 years ago, I was at a lost at what to do.

I had no direction, no idea on what to do next

It took a lot of time for me to compare and figure out which courses I could go

With this project, I hope to aid fellow students in their uni applications

FAQ

Q: Why aren't there selection of courses from SIT, SUTD and other universites?

A: The IGP for these universities are not found online


Q: Where did you get these figures/grades from?

A: I've linked my sources here: NUS, SMU and NTU


Q: How did you build this web app?

A: Frontend development was done using HTML and CSS while backend development was done using Flask. Deployment of web app is through Heroku.
